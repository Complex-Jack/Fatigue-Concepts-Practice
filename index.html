Shigley's Mechanical Engineering Design · Chapter 6

Fatigue Ground Balls

Widget 01

Fluctuating Stress Trainer

Convert Shigley-like load histories into alternating and mean stresses, including rotating bending and tension-compression cases.

Practice the stress bookkeeping before applying a fatigue criterion.
Widget 02

Fatigue Criterion Playground

Use provided Se, Sut, and Sy values to calculate infinite-life factor of safety with Goodman, Gerber, or Soderberg.

Includes a fatigue diagram and separate yielding check.
Widget 03

Finite-Life S-N Drill

Convert fluctuating stresses to equivalent reversed stress, then use the corrected S-N line to find fatigue life.

Emphasizes sequence: criterion first, S-N curve second.
